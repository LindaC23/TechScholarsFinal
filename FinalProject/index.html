Who was Sally Hemings?

Sally Hemings was an enslaved woman who made an extraordinary negotiation with Thomas Jefferson to gain freedom for her children. There are no known recordings of her appearance. However, there are descriptions; she was always described to have long straight hair and light-colored skin, and to be good-looking as well.
